Introduction
This page publishes the source codes of "Optimization benchmark problems for industrial applications" formulated by IEEJ investigating R&D committee on new development of computational intelligence techniques and their applications to industrial systems (Jan. 2011–Dec. 2012), IEEJ investigating R&D committee on system optimization and benchmark problems for industrial application (Jan. 2013–Dec. 2014, Apr. 2015–Mar. 2017), and IEEJ investigating Investigating R&D committee on computational intelligence techniques and benchmark problems for industrial application (Apr. 2017–).
In order to know specific formulations of P1, P2, P3, and P6, please see the corresponding manuscripts in the proceedings of SAMCON2015.

Benchmark problems
  • P1: Optimization benchmark problem for energy plant operational planning problem
    Based on Proc. of SAMCON2015, IS1-5
    Last update on June 5th, 2015
  • P2: Optimization benchmark problem for water supply pump scheduling
    Based on Proc. of SAMCON2015, IS1-4
    Last update on June 5th, 2015
  • P3: Optimization benchmark problem for operational planning and scheduling in an automatic picking system (Ver. 2) (Latest)
    Based on IEEJ Trans. on EIS, Vol. 135, No. 10 pp. 1270–1278
    Last update on Apr. 3rd, 2022
    • P3 (Ver. 1)
      Based on Proc. of Scheduling Symposium 2014, pp. 201–206
      Last update on Sept. 29th, 2015
  • P6: Optimization benchmark problem for serial-parallel switching of photovoltaic modules programming (Ver. 2) (Latest)
    Based on Proc. of SAMCON2015, IS1-3
    Last update on Nov. 19th, 2015
    • P6 (Ver. 1)
      Based on Proc. of the 2014 Annual Meeting on the Institute of Electrical Engineers of Japan, 4-S21(13)–(16)
      Last update on June 5th, 2015
